05 — Principles

Design principles

01

Typography is the design

No decorative elements needed. The serif creates warmth, the mono creates structure, the white space creates luxury.

02

Confident restraint

One accent color. One emphasis per section. If everything shouts, nothing is heard.

03

The italic is the voice

DM Serif Italic carries the emotional register — event names, emphasis, the human touch in an otherwise systematic grid.
