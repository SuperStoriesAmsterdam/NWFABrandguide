05 — Principles

RULES

01

THE TYPE IS THE POSTER

Bebas Neue at massive scale IS the visual identity. No illustration needed for impact. The condensed letterforms stack into compositions that work at any size.

02

GOLD IS THE SIGNAL

One accent per composition. Gold marks the word that matters — the date, the action, the name. If two things are gold, neither is special.

03

BORDERS MAKE THE GRID

2px black borders divide everything. No shadows, no radius, no cards. The structure is visible and honest. Like a poster grid or a newspaper layout.
