05_rules

Design principles

01

Mono is the identity

Space Mono at every scale. The monospace grid gives the brand its systematic, precise character. It reads like a wine label — technical, honest, no decoration.

02

Serif is the warmth

Source Serif italic is the human voice inside the system. Descriptions, stories, the parts where you hear a person speaking — not a database outputting.

03

The underscore is alive

The trailing underscore is the brand's punctuation. It means: still going, still fermenting, still growing. Festival_ not Festival.

04

Green means living

Green is for natural process — fermentation, growth, the vineyard. Used on the accent word, the active state, the signal that something is happening now.
